TU Dublin Irish language classes and online training for 2020

A new year of Free Irish Language Classes on campus and on-line for TU Dublin Staff and Students will begin from Monday 3rd February 2020 organised by Oifig na Gaeilge, TU Dublin’s Irish language office and Gaelchultúr Teo, supported by Staff Development.

CAO Information Evenings - Thursday, 16 January

Technological University Dublin (TU Dublin) will host CAO information evenings for parents and guardians of school leavers, mature students and QQI (PLC) students on Thursday, 16 January in Grangegorman, Tallaght and Blanchardstown.

TU Dublin Global Graduate Art Programme

As part of the TU Dublin ‘Global Graduate Art Programme’, a painting by Bethany Garvey-Williams will soon hang in the Irish Embassy in Bogota, as a gift from the University.

Erasmus+ training on campus for TU Dublin staff

An information and training session took place in Grangegorman today on the Erasmus+ International Credit Mobility (ICM) application process. The half-day event was attended by 16 members of TU Dublin staff and was facilitated by consultant Mr Paul Guest, a former Erasmus project assessor with the European Commission.
